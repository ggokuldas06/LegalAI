MASTER PROFESSIONAL SERVICES & TECHNOLOGY INTEGRATION AGREEMENT  
Effective Date: January 10, 2024  

This Master Professional Services & Technology Integration Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between:

Client: Horizon Biomedical Research Institute (“Client”)  
Service Provider: Vertex Systems Integrations, LLC (“Service Provider”)  

WHEREAS, Client conducts advanced medical-device R&D requiring secure data handling and specialized software systems;  
WHEREAS, Service Provider provides professional consulting, data-engineering, and systems-integration services;  
NOW, THEREFORE, the Parties agree as follows:

--------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — DEFINITIONS
--------------------------------------
1.1 “Deliverables” means analytics dashboards, automated workflows, integration scripts, documentation, and other work products developed under this Agreement.
1.2 “Confidential Information” means all non-public scientific, technical, financial, operational, or strategic information disclosed orally or in writing.
1.3 “Background Technology” includes tools, libraries, code frameworks, and methodologies developed independently by Service Provider prior to the Effective Date.
1.4 “Project Schedule” refers to the timeline set forth in Exhibit B.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — SCOPE OF SERVICES
--------------------------------------
2.1 Service Provider shall perform:  
(a) data-ingestion pipeline development;  
(b) cross-platform device integration;  
(c) real-time anomaly detection setup;  
(d) cloud-to-local replication configuration;  
(e) compliance-aligned system documentation.

2.2 Services will be delivered in three phases:
- **Phase I – Architecture Review & Planning** (6 weeks)  
- **Phase II – Development & Integration** (14 weeks)  
- **Phase III – Validation, Testing & Knowledge Transfer** (10 weeks)

2.3 Weekly progress meetings shall be held with the Client’s Technical Director.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — PAYMENT TERMS
--------------------------------------
3.1 Fees. Client shall compensate Service Provider at **$250/hour** for engineering work and **$300/hour** for compliance-oriented consulting.  
3.2 Monthly Invoicing. Invoices must include itemized time logs.  
3.3 Late Payments. Any invoice not paid within **30 days** accrues **1.25% monthly interest**.  
3.4 Travel Expenses. Reasonable travel expenses are reimbursable with receipts.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 4 — CONFIDENTIALITY
--------------------------------------
4.1 Both Parties must maintain strict confidentiality over Confidential Information.  
4.2 Confidentiality obligations survive for **5 years** following termination.  
4.3 Forbidden Activities. Parties shall not:  
(a) disclose scientific research data to third parties;  
(b) decompile or reverse engineer proprietary tools;  
(c) store Client data in non-approved cloud environments.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 5 — INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
--------------------------------------
5.1 Client owns all Deliverables.  
5.2 Service Provider retains rights to Background Technology.  
5.3 Licensing. Service Provider grants Client a **perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license** to use Background Technology strictly as embedded within Deliverables.  
5.4 Open Source. Service Provider must disclose use of any GPL or copyleft components.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 6 — DATA SECURITY & COMPLIANCE
--------------------------------------
6.1 Systems must comply with:  
(a) HIPAA data-protection standards;  
(b) NIST SP 800-53 security controls;  
(c) Client's internal security protocols.

6.2 Service Provider shall implement:  
- network segmentation;  
- encryption in transit and at rest;  
- audit logging;  
- quarterly penetration testing.

6.3 Any security incident must be reported within **24 hours**.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 7 — WARRANTIES
--------------------------------------
7.1 Service Provider warrants:  
(a) Deliverables will conform to specifications in Exhibit A;  
(b) services will be performed professionally;  
(c) no intentional malware or harmful code will be introduced.

7.2 Disclaimer. Except as stated, Deliverables are provided “AS-IS.”

--------------------------------------
SECTION 8 — INDEMNIFICATION
--------------------------------------
8.1 Service Provider indemnifies Client for:  
(a) IP infringement claims arising from Deliverables;  
(b) data breaches caused by Service Provider;  
(c) gross negligence or willful misconduct.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 9 — LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
--------------------------------------
9.1 Neither Party is liable for consequential damages.  
9.2 Aggregate liability cap: **fees paid in the previous 6 months**.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 10 — TERMINATION
--------------------------------------
10.1 Either Party may terminate with **30 days’ notice**.  
10.2 Immediate termination applies in case of material breach.  
10.3 Upon termination, Client must pay for all hours worked.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 11 — GOVERNING LAW
--------------------------------------
11.1 Governed by the laws of California.  
11.2 Venue: San Mateo County.

--------------------------------------
SECTION 12 — MISCELLANEOUS
--------------------------------------
12.1 Assignment requires written consent.  
12.2 Amendments must be in writing.  
12.3 Entire Agreement supersedes prior agreements.
