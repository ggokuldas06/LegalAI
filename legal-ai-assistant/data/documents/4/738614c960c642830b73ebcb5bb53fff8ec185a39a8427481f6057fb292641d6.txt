SECTION 47 — DISCLOSURE REQUIREMENTS  
47(a) Businesses must clearly disclose material terms, pricing structures, and renewal conditions.  
47(b) Disclosures shall appear in a format reasonably understandable to the average consumer.  
47(c) Omission of significant terms constitutes deceptive conduct.

SECTION 48 — WARRANTY RIGHTS  
48(a) Implied warranties of merchantability and fitness for purpose apply automatically unless lawfully disclaimed.  
48(b) Express warranties must be honored strictly as represented.  
48(c) Repairs must be completed within a reasonable time.

SECTION 49 — REMEDIES FOR DEFECTIVE GOODS  
49(a) Consumers may seek refund, repair, or replacement.  
49(b) If defects persist after two repair attempts, a refund becomes mandatory.  
49(c) Claims must be processed within 20 business days.

SECTION 50 — PROHIBITED PRACTICES  
50(a) Businesses may not misrepresent product capabilities.  
50(b) Hidden fees, surcharges, and post-contract price increases are prohibited.  
50(c) Coercive sales tactics invalidate agreements formed under duress.

SECTION 51 — CIVIL PENALTIES  
51(a) Violations may incur fines up to $25,000 per infraction.  
51(b) Repeated violations within a 12-month period constitute aggravated offenses.  
51(c) Courts may award attorney’s fees to prevailing consumers.
